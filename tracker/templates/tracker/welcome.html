BeerQuest - A Journey to Victory and Mass Beer Knowledge. It is a B(eer) Odyssey.

Welcome to BeerQuest. Our goal is to drink beer and be able to remember which ones we liked. We use a two prong rating system, flavor and volume. Flavor tracks the overall taste and enjoyment of the beer, a higher value means a more pleasant beer experience. Volume indicates how much of a beer you could drink. Some beers may be very tasty, but you may only be able to drink a few of them. The drunakability of a beer is then calculated based on the volume and the ABV.
